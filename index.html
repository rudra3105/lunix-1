How it work

From Raw Material to Finished Dose – Our Manufacturing Process

At Lunix Pharma, we follow a structured and quality-first approach to manufacturing high-efficacy nutraceuticals and pharmaceuticals. From sourcing premium-grade raw materials to packaging compliant finished products, our end-to-end workflow ensures consistency, safety, and global export-readiness.

Step 01

We procure scientifically validated, pharma-grade raw materials from trusted suppliers, with strict identity and purity checks.

Step 02
