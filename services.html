Key Research Fields

Leading Innovation Across Core Fields of Pharmaceutical Research

At Lunix Pharma, our research spans across diverse domains to develop safe, effective, and future-ready health solutions. Each field is backed by expert scientists, global standards, and a passion for innovation — driving real-world healthcare impact.

Nutraceutical Innovation

Advancing dietary supplements and functional foods to support wellness, immunity, and preventive health.
